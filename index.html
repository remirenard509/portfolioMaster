Details about the apprenticeship at Coda Orléans

I am looking for an apprenticeship in the field of software development, regardless of the sector: front-end, back-end, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, or any related area.
I am available throughout France, preferably in the Paris region, Centre-Val de Loire, or for a remote position. I am looking for a two-year contract starting in September 2025. The rhythm of the apprenticeship is three weeks in the company and one week at school.
Tuition fees are €8,500 per year. In the case of an apprenticeship contract for the Fullstack Developer Bachelor, your OPCO covers €7,178 of the tuition fees, leaving €1,322 to be covered per year of training.
In addition, the State grants companies hiring apprentices with disabilities a €6,000 aid. Since I have RQTH status, I am eligible for this aid, which can be combined with other specific aids.
Beyond the €6,000 hiring bonus for an apprentice with a disability, you may also benefit from additional support schemes.
